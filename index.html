GOOGLE SHEETS SYNC SETUP
Step 1 of 4

Create a Google Sheet

Your data will be stored in a free Google Sheet that all IT staff can access.

  1. Go to sheets.google.com
  2. Click + Blank to create a new spreadsheet
  3. Name it ITAssetTrack Data
Step 2 of 4

Add the Sync Script

Open Extensions → Apps Script, delete any existing code, paste this, then click Save (💾).

Code.gs
function doGet(e){return handleRequest(e);} function doPost(e){return handleRequest(e);} // Click "Copy Code" above for the full script
Step 3 of 4

Deploy as Web App

In Apps Script click Deploy → New deployment. Set: Type = Web app, Execute as = Me, Who has access = Anyone. Click Deploy, authorise, copy the URL.

Step 4 of 4

🎉 You're Connected!

ITAssetTrack will now sync data to your Google Sheet automatically every 30 seconds.

✅ Devices sync automatically
✅ Local cache keeps the app working offline
✅ Share the Sheet URL with your IT team
